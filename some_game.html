Playing Some Game

Phase 0: Not your turn

There are no mandatory actions in this phase

Your options:
* Offer a trade to the player whose turn it is
* My turn has come

Phase 1: Before roll

There are no mandatory actions in this phase

Your options:
* Play a development card
* Enter Dice roll phase

Phase 2: Roll the dice

Roll two dice and choose the option based on your result

Your options:
* Rolled 7
* Rolled other thab 7

Phase 3: Robber variant

1. Every player with more than 7 cards discards floor(no_cards/2) cards, you included.

2. Choose a spot to place the robber - it must move

3. Steal a random card from a chosen player now neighboring the robber

Your options:
Enter trade-build phase

Phase 3: Resources variant

Every player claims a card for rolled resources not blocked by robber - 1 for village, 2 for city

Your options:
Enter trade-build phase

Phase 4: Trade and build

There are no mandatory actions in this phase

Your options:
* Play a development card
* Build something
* Trade something
* Buy a development card
* End your turn

END GAME
